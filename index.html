BEFORE YOU START YOUR REGISTRATION, PLEASE READ THIS IMPORTANT MESSAGE ON HOW TO MAKE PAYMENT SUCCESSFULLY Follow these steps to register for an examination.
  1. If this is your first time of using this portal, you will need to create an account on the portal,
  2. If you have already created an account, what you need to do is to login, skip this step if you have already logged in
  3. After logging in, you will need to create a profile and upload your passport.
  4. After the above step, you can now proceed by clicking on "Apply for Exam"
  5. Then Select the examination you want to apply for, click on "Submit"
  6. You will be redirected to the examination summary page, click on "Proceed to Payment" button, you can select from any of the two options of payment available.
  7. After a successful payment, you would be redirected to the Application Form(IF YOU ARE NOT REDIRECTED TO THE EXAMINATION FORM AFTER A SUCCESSFUL PAYMENT, PLEASE SEND YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS TO +2348161549282), Please read the instructions carefully at the top of the application form.
  8. Fill the examination application form and attached required documents/information, then click on the preview button, confirm all information then Click on "Submit" button to submit your application.
PRIMARY CANDIDATES DO NOT NEED TO SEND ANY DOCUMENT TO THE COLLEGE WHEN THEY ARE DONE WITH SUCCESSFUL REGISTRATION AND PAYMENT. DIPLOMA, PART I AND PART II CANDIDATES ARE TO CHECK THE EXAMINATION ADVERT TO KNOW THE DOCUMENTS THEY ARE TO SEND TO THE COLLEGE.   • Certificate for full Registration with National Medical Board
  • Certificates for Voluntary Service(NYSC or others where applicable)
  • Evidence of Change of Name(Where applicable)

HOW TO REGISTER FOR A COURSE.

  • Login https://www.wacscoac.com at if you have already created an account, if not, you will need to create an account here https://www.wacscoac.com/register
  • After sucessful login, it is compulsory that you create your profile.
  • After creating your profile, click on the "Course Registration" box on the dash board, You would be redirected to a page that contains list of available courses.
  • Click on the course you wish to register for, select your faculty and type your institution, Then proceed to payment.
